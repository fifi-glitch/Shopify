How much do you know about the SKZ? This IMPOSSIBLE Stray Kids Quiz is going to challenge you. Only a real STAY can score 20 out of 20. Let’s find out.

The SKZ Quiz That You Will Fail

You might know many things about the Korean boys’ band that survived the 2017 JYP survival show. But the chances are pretty thin that you can answer all questions of this SKZ trivia quiz correctly. Scoring more than 16 on this test makes you the ultimate STAY.

Unlike Stray Kids Bias Quizzes or personality matches, this one challenges your knowledge about the group. And the fun fact is, less than 40% of the participants can beat it.

The Most Challenging Song and Lyrics Questions

Fans love to memorize the band’s lyrics. But let’s see how many of the lines you actually remember under pressure. Our trivia test includes some of the most confusing missed-lyric types of questions you could find on the internet.

Of course, the real Stay would find it an easy-peasy task to complete the lines. But the question is, are you one? Can you beat the test without asking for Siri’s help?

How Much Do You Know the Stray Kids Members?

SKZ has eight adorable and talented members, Bang, Lee, Changbin, Hyunjin, Jisung, Felix, Seungmin, and Jeongin. The quiz includes questions at least two personal questions about each of them. So, you need to know more than your bias’s favorite food and song to beat the game.

What Does Your SKZ Quiz Score Mean?

The perfect score is 20. But the points you earn for each correct answer increase your chance to get the ultimate title, the real Stay. (Here is what your test result means).

· 0-5: K-Pop Enthusiast

It seems like you know some stuff about Stray Kids. But so many pieces of information are waiting for you to catch up on. Do not get disappointed by your score, though. You can always increase your engagement with the fandom and learn new stuff about the boys.

· 6-10: Newbie Fan

You did a good job. Although such a score does not make you the real STAY, it is still a satisfying result. It is obvious that you recently joined the fandom. Sidenote: come back a couple of weeks later, and you will surprise yourself with much higher scores.

You almost beat the Stray Kids Quiz. Having at least 11 correct answers on the test is something to be proud of—as the questions are mind-bending. You have undeniable potential to earn the REAL STAY title. But as Felix says, “Just a little braver.” That is all it takes for you to get a perfect score the next time you take the test.

· 16-20: The REAL STAY

If you scored more than sixteen points on the SKZ Quiz, you are the guru. Less than 40% of the participants and fans manage to achieve such a result. And, in fact, less than 10% get a perfect score. So, you can definitely be proud of your performance. The boys would surely be proud of you.

5 Fun Facts to Warm Up Before Taking the Test

One year after the band’s debut, the boys played the “Who’s Who?” game on BuzzFeed Celeb. And they answered questions about what each member is known for and who does what. Here is a summary of the funniest and cutest things we found out about the eight handsome boys.

  1. Hyunjin is obsessed with selfies.

When asked, “Who takes the most selfies?” everyone pointed out Hwang. And he was open about his love for posting on social media and keeping the Stay updated.

  1. Felix is a real-deal gamer.

Did you know that Yongbuk has been a gamer since he was a teenager? The band members all agree that Felix spends most of his time with video games, and he constantly encourages others to join him.

  1. Bang is always writing songs.

No matter where he is, Chang is constantly thinking about writing lyrics and making new songs. That is why he is one of the three main producers of the band.

  1. Jisung forgets dance moves and lyrics.

Han is probably the funniest boy in the group. But he is also known as the most forgetful member. According to other guys, Jisung constantly forgets his choreography and lines. But everyone adores him no matter what.

  1. Lee Know is usually texting (or playing with his phone).

Know loves his cellphone. So, the boys are not surprised to find him texting his mom or playing games somewhere in the corner.
